1. **Visit the homepage** at modelmix.app

3. **You'll see the AI comparison interface** ready for your first prompt

That's it! You get one free question to try before creating an account.

---

## üìù How to Use ModelMix

### Step 1: Choose Your AI Models

At the top of the screen, you'll see model selector buttons. 

- **Click a slot** to pick which AI model goes there
- **Choose 1-10 models** to compare at once
- **Popular choices**: GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Llama

**Tip**: Start with 2-3 models to keep things manageable!

### Step 2: Type Your Question

In the big text box at the bottom:
- Type any question, task, or prompt
- Be as specific as you want
- Press **Enter** or click **Run Models** to send

### Step 3: Compare the Responses

Each model's response appears in its own panel:
- **Watch responses stream in** real-time
- **Scroll within each panel** if responses are long
- **Copy any response** with the copy button

### Step 4: Continue the Conversation

After getting responses, you can:
- **Ask a follow-up question** in the same conversation
- **@mention a specific model** to ask just that one
- **Start fresh** with the "New" button

## üë§ Creating an Account

After your free question, you'll need an account to continue.

### To Sign Up:
1. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Get Started"**
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Check your email for confirmation (or it may auto-confirm)
4. You're in! You'll receive starter credits

### Credits System
- Each question costs **1 credit per model**
- Asking 3 models = 3 credits
- You get **free credits** when you sign up
- More credits available through upgrades

## üÜò Need Help?

### Common Questions

**Q: Why do some models give different length answers?**  
A: Each AI has its own style! Some are concise, others more detailed.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**  
A: Yes! ModelMix works on mobile browsers.

**Q: What if a model fails to respond?**  
A: Sometimes models timeout. Try again or select a different model.

**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: We don't store your conversations. Check our Privacy Policy for details.
